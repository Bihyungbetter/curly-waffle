Dish Spotlight

Take a closer look at our chef's special creations

Lemongrass Tofu Stir-Fry

Our Signature Dish

Our chef's masterpiece combines crispy tofu with aromatic lemongrass, colorful bell peppers, and fragrant Thai basil. The tofu is marinated overnight to absorb all the rich flavors before being perfectly seared.

Coconut Green Curry

Customer Favorite

Our aromatic green curry features seasonal vegetables simmered in rich coconut milk with Thai basil, lemongrass, and galangal. Each batch of curry paste is freshly made in-house using traditional techniques.

Mushroom Dumplings

Handcrafted Perfection

Our delicate dumplings are filled with a savory mixture of shiitake, king oyster, and enoki mushrooms, along with cabbage and aromatic herbs. Each dumpling is handcrafted and steamed to perfection.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Farm to Table

At Time for Thyme, we partner with local farmers to bring you the freshest, most sustainable ingredients possible. Our produce comes from farms within a 50-mile radius of Charlotte, reducing our carbon footprint and supporting local agriculture.

Zero Waste Initiative

We're committed to minimizing waste through composting, recycling, and thoughtful sourcing. Our to-go containers are 100% compostable, and we've eliminated single-use plastics from our operations.
